Muhammed Ali

One of the greatest boxers of all time

Timeline of Ali's life

  • 1942 - Born in Louisville, Kentucky
  • 1954 - Won his amateur boxing debut against Ronnie O'Keefe
  • 1960 - Competed in the Summer Olympics and won the light heavyweight gold medal
  • 1960 - Won his professional debut against Tunney Hunsaker
  • 1962 - Won his fight against his former trainer, Archie Moore
  • 1964 - Beat Sonny Liston, winning the heavyweight title at the young age of 22
  • 1967 - Refused to be drafted to the Vietnam War, resulting in his arrest
  • 1971 - Lost the "Fight of the Century" against Joe Frazier
  • 2016 - Dies at the age of 74
